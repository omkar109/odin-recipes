Honey Sriracha Tofu

Description

This recipe produces firm, crisp tofu that is covered in a honey-sriracha glaze. This tofu is served well with rice or some sort of grains

Ingredients

  • tofu
  • sriracha
  • honey
  • soy sauce
  • crushed red pepper
  • cooking oil
  • green onions
  • corn starch

Steps

  1. Remove moisture from tofu
  2. Combine the sriracha, honey, soy sauce and red pepper in a bowl
  3. Cut the tofu into .5 inch cubes
  4. Whisk soy sauce into a bowl of oil, then add cubed tofu and stir until tofu is coated
  5. Sprinkle cornstarch onto the tofu and stir again
  6. Heat a skillet, add the prepared tofu until it is brown and crispy on all sides
  7. Pour the honey sriracha sauce on the tofu and cook for another 3 minutes
  8. Top the tofu with slices green onions and serve
